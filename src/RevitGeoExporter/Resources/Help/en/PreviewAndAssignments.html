Preview and Assignments

The preview window renders prepared export geometry without writing GeoPackage files. It helps verify units, openings, details, levels, warnings, and vertical circulation output.

What you can inspect

  • Layer toggles for units, openings, details, and levels.
  • Warnings, overridden floors, and unassigned floor-derived units.
  • Per-feature metadata by clicking the canvas.

Floor category assignments

Unassigned floor-derived units can be grouped by floor type name and assigned to supported categories. These overrides are exporter-side project settings and do not rename or rewrite Revit elements.

Navigation

  • Mouse wheel zooms.
  • Drag to pan.
  • Use Fit and Reset to control the view.

See Settings and Profiles for where project mappings are stored.
